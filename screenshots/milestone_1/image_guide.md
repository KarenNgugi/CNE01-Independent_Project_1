**1.10.-add-to-company.png:** successful output after adding all the newly created users to the `company` group

**1.12.-add-to-ict-team.png:** successful output after adding users `admin1`, `dev1`, `dev2`, and `tester1` to group `ict_team`

**1.13.-validating-user-groups.png:** successful output after confirming a random sample of the newly-created users belonged to their respective groups

**1.14.-chgrp-ict-team.png:** successful output after changing group ownership of all project files to `ict_team`

**1.15.-chgrp-company.png:** successful output after changing group ownership of `project_summary.md` to `company`, and assigning user ownership of all project files (except 'project notes.docx') to `admin1`

**1.16.-chmod-files.png:** successful output after changing the permissions of all directories to 774 (user and group have full permissions, others have read only permissions) and of `project_summary.md` to 640(user can read and write, group can read only, others have zero permissions)
